Reference Page: Everything You Need To Know!

Welcome to our Webmaster project, Saffron Spice! This is the TSA required reference page.

For this project, we coded in HTML and supplemented with CSS. We affirm that we also employed the use of a web framework, Bootstrap, to make some aspects of the website design easier to code. However, every aspect of the website was originally designed and coded by us.

All graphics and illustrations used in our website were available to the public domain, free for use. Our logo and homepage divider were created in canva, and our homepage display photo was taken from Pexels (credit to pexels.com/@chanwalrus/).
